Component: Playlist
Wavesurfer
Service

  • WaveSurfer as a service

    Utilizing a service to load WaveSurfer, and rendering the waveform on an element in a dedicated component.

  • Media control injection

    Any component that injects the service is able to play and control the WaveSurfer player.

  • Persistent audio player

    As long as the player component exists outside of the router outlet, audio will be persistent and will play uninterrupted, even as the visitor navigates to other routes. 

        <router-outlet></router-outlet>
    <app-player></app-player>
  • Each item in the playlist handles clicks with the Wavesurfer Service's playTrack method: 
(click)="ws.playTrack(playitem.url, playitem.trackid, playitem.title)"
