Component: Playlist
Wavesurfer
Service
  • We can build a persistent audio player by utilizing a service to load WaveSurfer, and rendering the waveform via an element in a dedicated component.
  • Any component that injects the service can then user the service to play a track.
  • As long as this dedicated component exists outside of the router outlet, audio will be persistent and will play uninterrupted, even as the visitor navigates to other routes 

        <router-outlet></router-outlet>
    <app-player></app-player>
  • Each item in the playlist handles clicks with the Wavesurfer Service's playTrack method: 
(click)="ws.playTrack(playitem.url, playitem.trackid, playitem.title)"
